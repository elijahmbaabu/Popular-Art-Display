Poster With Vectors

A poster with vectors is a type of poster that uses vector graphics as its primary design element. Vector graphics are images that are composed of mathematical objects, such as lines, curves, and polygons, rather than pixels. This makes them scalable to any size without losing quality. As a result, vector graphics are often used for high-resolution printing, such as for posters.
